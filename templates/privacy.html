Privacy Policy

Welcome to LimbRevive, an innovative iOS application designed to help individuals with amputations find relief from phantom limb pain using Augmented Reality (AR) technology. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information and the steps we take to protect it.

Information We Do Not Collect

LimbRevive does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. All data you provide is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

Data You Provide

To enhance your experience, you may provide the following information, which is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Amputation details (e.g., which leg the amputation is on)

Permissions

LimbRevive may request the following permissions to provide specific features within the app:

  • Microphone and Speech Recognition: These permissions are required to enable voice commands for controlling the virtual limb. Voice data is processed on your device and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Camera: This permission is necessary to enable AR services within the application. The camera is used to place the virtual limb in your environment by detecting image tags. No camera data is stored or transmitted.

How We Use Your Data

All data you provide and permissions you grant are solely used to enhance your user experience within the LimbRevive application. Specifically:

  • Your personal data (name, age, gender, amputation details) helps customize the virtual limb and make the application more effective for your needs.
  • Microphone and speech recognition data are used to interpret your voice commands for controlling the virtual limb.
  • Camera data is used to implement AR functionality, allowing the virtual limb to be placed accurately in your environment.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your data is secure. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security measures, such as passcodes and biometric authentication. We recommend that you keep your device secure to protect your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through an update in the application. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or LimbRevive's practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@limbrevive.com

Conclusion

Your privacy is important to us. By using LimbRevive, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We are dedicated to providing you with a safe and effective application to help manage phantom limb pain while ensuring your data remains private and secure.
